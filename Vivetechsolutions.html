About Project

Vive Tech Solutions is a leading digital marketing agency that helps businesses grow through technical expertise and data-driven strategies. While they were experiencing high demand for their services, the company's existing website lacked modern features and functionality needed to showcase their full-service offerings. They enlisted Webiz to build a next-generation website from scratch.

Challenges

Design was kicked off with styling moodboards reflecting the brand's success-driven positioning. Wireframes established an intuitive user flow between sections. Prototype testing optimized the navigation and ensured self-explanatory calls to action. Development focused on clean code practices for high performance.

Features:

The launched site comprised individually crafted service pages visually summarizing: Specific challenges addressed
Tailored methodologies and deliverables
Relevant case studies with quantified results
Expert teams leading each discipline
Integrated contact forms to book consultations
Additional resources like ebooks, webinars and calculators enhanced perceived value. Analytics provided insights into most engaging elements.

Results

Since launch, traffic to the services section has grown 300% as Vive Tech efficiently directs prospects to discover solutions addressing their unique needs. 60% of visitors request consultations after exploring 3+ specialized pages.
